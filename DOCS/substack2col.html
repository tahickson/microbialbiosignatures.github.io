Spain

The Sexenio Revolution and the First Republic: 1868-74

Overthrow of the Monarchy and a period of relatively liberal policies in Spain. Pisses off conservative religious folks who see policies as a threat.

The Bourbon Restoration: 1874-1931

A coup d'etat brings back monarchical rule. From 1923-1930, Spain ruled by a military dictator. I'd say pretty conservative. Conservative religious folk get their sense of power back.

The Second Republic: 1931-1939

A republican and socialist government is elected in what are generally considered fair elections. Pretty liberal. Pisses off conservative religious folks who see policies as a threat. It is true that many Catholic priests and other poobahs were assassinated by Republican sympathizers.

Franco regime: 1936-1975

Fascist and very conservative. Conservative religious folk get their sense of power back.

Post Franco

Variable in terms of liberal to conservative.
