Welcome!

We are a group of geoscientists working to create a database of microbialite biosignatures. This includes:

  • Morphological characteristics
  • Textural traits
  • Geochemical properties

The traces of microbial metabolic processes in the rock record are, to us, equivalent to primary sedimentary structures and/or trace fossils. They require thorough, non-genetic descriptions that we can then be interpreted in terms of physical, chemical and biological processes. For this reason, our database makes extensive use of Handbook for the Study of Microbialites (Awramik and Grey, 2020) , the first detailed non-genetic classification of microbialites to date.
